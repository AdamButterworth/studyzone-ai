# StudyZone AI — Landing Page Plan

## Design Direction

**Content inspiration**: YouLearn.ai (what to say, feature framing, social proof structure)
**Visual style**: Craft.do (how it looks and feels)

### Craft Design Language to Replicate
- **Background**: Warm off-white/cream (`#FFF8F0`-ish), NOT pure white
- **Typography**: Large, elegant serif headings (editorial feel) + clean sans-serif body text
- **Color palette**: Soft pastels — light blue, lavender, pale yellow, mint green, peach. Each section gets its own accent color. No harsh primaries.
- **Cards/Boxes**: Large rounded corners, soft shadows, layered/overlapping card compositions. Cards often contain mini product screenshots or illustrations.
- **Decorative elements**: Soft watercolor-style cloud/paint blob illustrations floating in backgrounds. Organic, hand-crafted feel.
- **Section spacing**: Very generous whitespace between sections. Content breathes.
- **Testimonials**: Large italic serif quotes with small avatar + name to the right
- **CTAs**: Black pill-shaped buttons (primary), white/outline pill buttons (secondary)
- **Overall feel**: Premium, warm, approachable, editorial — NOT cold/techy/startup-y

## Implementation Notes

### Fonts
- **Headings**: A serif with personality — consider: Playfair Display, Fraunces, or Newsreader (Google Fonts, free). Craft uses a custom serif but these are close.
- **Body**: Inter or DM Sans (clean, modern sans-serif)

### Animations
- Subtle fade-in on scroll for each section (intersection observer)
- Cards slightly float up as they enter viewport
- Hero product screenshot can have a subtle parallax or float effect
- Keep it tasteful — Craft is not heavy on animation, just smooth transitions

### Responsive
- Hero stacks vertically on mobile
- Feature cards go to single column
- Persona cards become horizontal scroll
- Nav collapses to hamburger
- All generous spacing scales down proportionally

### Assets Needed
- Product screenshots (we'll create these as we build the app, use mockups initially)
- 5 persona photos (can use AI-generated or stock)
- Decorative cloud/blob SVGs (watercolor style, 3-4 variations)
- Icon set for features (Lucide or Heroicons)
- Logo for StudyZone AI
